Test Two

SRT — Situation Reaction Test

60 real-world scenarios in 30 minutes — about 30 seconds each. The short clock is deliberate: it wants your first honest thought. Learn to recognise the dilemma structure, and you respond in seconds instead of freezing.

  1. 1SRT BasicsMechanics, the quantity-vs-quality question settled, and the Assess → Decide → Act engine.
  2. 2The Five Dilemma StructuresEmergency, ethical, interpersonal, team, and personal — recognise the structure, respond fast.
  3. 3The Double-Bind TrapSituations built so either choice looks wrong — and the signature move to escape them.
  4. 4Deep Dive: Ethical DilemmaDuty vs loyalty — acting on principle through the proper channel.
  5. 5Deep Dive: Competing PrioritiesHold both fronts, sacrifice neither — delegation and partial coverage.
  6. 6Practice Worksheet16 tagged situations, ~30-second timer, self-audit at the end. Interactive trainer.
